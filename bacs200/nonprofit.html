Statement of Faith

We believe that the church exists to glorify God, by proclaiming the good news of Jesus Christ, and equipping God's people to live in light of that good news.

Core Values

We believe that:

  • The Bible is the inspired word of God given to us through men who came from God and were inspired by the Holy Spirit. (2 Timothy 3:16, 2 Peter 1:21)
  • All have sinned and fall short of the glory of God. (Romans 3:23)
  • The wages of sin is death, but the gift of God is eternal life through Christ Jesus (Romans 6:23).
  • Jesus Christ, who was one with God, fully God, and fully man, was born of a virgin, experienced every temptation we will ever experience, yet lived a completely sinless life, died for all of humanity as a substitutionary sacrifice, and rose again on the third day. (John 10:30, John 1:1, Luke 1:26-35, Hebrews 2:18, 1 John 3:5, 1 John 2:2 and Isaiah 53:5-6, Matthew 28:6 and 1 Peter 1:3)
  • We can be justified (made right with God) freely through the gift of grace from Jesus Christ, who paid for all our sins with His own blood, when we recieve Jesus and beleive in His name. (Romans 2:24, Ephesians 1:7, John 1:12)
  • When we recieve Jesus, the Holy Spirit (part of the trinity of God) comes into us and changes us from our sinful ways so that we can live life in response to God's goodness towards us. (Acts 5:3-4, Titus 3:5, Romans 8:9, Ephesians 2:8-9)
  • The Holy Spirit also seals us until the day that Jesus comes back to get us or we die, whichever comes first. (Ephesians 1:13-14, John 10:23-30, Romans 8:38-39, Romans 14:9, 1 Peter 1:5, Jude 24)
  • We are loved by God as is shown by Him sending His own Son Jesus to die for us, so that we can become the children of God. (1 John 4:9, 1 John 3:1)
  • As we loved so much by God, we are told to go into the world and make disciples of all nations, to baptize them in the name of the Father, Son, and the Holy Spirit, and to teach them to obey everything God has commanded us, for surely He is with us always until the end of time! (Matthew 28:19-20)
